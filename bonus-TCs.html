1. Termonology

The following are common bonus terminology used in the description of bonuses:

Activation: when a bonus is activated and any resulting bonus cash is added to your bonus balance

Available Bonuses: bonuses and/or bonus that you are either eligible to be awarded when making a deposit or that have been awarded to you but have not yet activated by you.

Awarding: when a bonus is added to a player’s account for use.

Bonus type: this is an insurance award, credited at the discretion of the casino, based on losses during a specific period of time.

Deposit Bonus: a bonus that requires a deposit to be made to claim that is typically a percentage of the amount deposited by the player.

Eligibility: if a player is eligible to be awarded a bonus at the discretion of UrbanCasino

Forfeit: when a player requests for a bonus to be canceled or cancels the use of the bonus themselves.

Free Bonus: a bonus that requires no deposit to claim and make use of.

Free Spins: a bonus that credits free spins on a game of the casinos choosing with no deposit required.

Loyalty Cash Bonus: a cash credit made at the discretion of the casino that comes with no restrictions and can be used to play any game in the casino.

Period of validity or Expiry date: is the number of days a player has to play through the wagering requirements of the bonus. Any remaining bonus and winnings resulting from the bonus play, will expire and be voided after the number of predetermined days have lapsed.

Redeem or Claim: once the wagering requirements are met, any bonus balance and/or winnings is moved and/or converted to the players cash balance..

Wagering Requirements: is the amount of wagering needed before a player’s bonus cash is moved to their cash balance, as an example: – On a 100% bonus with 10 x wagering requirement, this means the player will get up to a $100 bonus match on their first deposit. If the player deposits $100, You will receive an instant bonus match of 100% of $100. To turn the bonus money into real money to then make a withdrawal, the player will need to wager the deposited amount 10 times resulting in total bets of $1,000.

Welcome Bonus: is the first set of bonuses offered which bets will contribute to the wagering requirements.
